Voice Commands

The NETRA Chatbot supports voice commands via the microphone icon. Ensure your browser has microphone permission enabled.

  • "Status" - Get a summary of active alerts and officer status.
  • "Alerts" - List the most recent unresolved alerts.
  • "Show me officers" - List currently active security personnel.

Dashboard Features

Sharing Alerts

You can now share critical alerts directly via WhatsApp or Email. Click the "Share" button on any alert card or table row.

Tactical Map

Use the toggle button to switch between satellite view and heatmap view to identify high-activity zones.

Messages

Use the Messages tab to communicate with the Administrator or broadcast messages to other officers in the field.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. An OTP will be sent to your registered email.

Why is my account pending?

New accounts require Admin approval. Please contact your supervisor if approval is delayed.

Voice commands aren't working.

Voice features currently require Google Chrome. Please check your microphone settings.
